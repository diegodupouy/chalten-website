Story

Rooted in entrepreneurship & inspired by Chaltén.

Chalten SA was founded after a decade spent building Lunaphore from an EPFL spin-off into a global spatial biology company, acquired by Bio-Techne in 2023. Having experienced the full journey from prototype to industrial product, we now support founders combining scientific depth with ambitious execution.

The name Chalten comes from Cerro Chaltén (Fitz Roy) in Patagonia: a reminder to keep clarity of vision, respect for the environment, and resilience on the way to the summit.
